## 快招加盟的完整收割闭环

1. **线索诱引**  
   你在搜索引擎查找知名品牌，点进带有“授权认证、限额加盟”的宣传页。留下电话后，销售告知：“全省仅剩最后一个开放名额。”
2. **现场锁单**  
   受邀赶赴总部样板间。现场人头攒动，签约喜讯不断。销售催促：“今天付定金立减 5 万，今晚截止。”
3. **选址下套**  
   签完合同，选址经理推荐你去品牌大店旁边开铺，中间赚取二房东差价。
4. **甩锅离场**  
   三个月后现金流枯竭，总部复核给出一句话：“商业模式没问题，是你经营不到位。”

整个流程没有暴力强迫，每一步都设计了精确的心理博弈。

## 你正面临哪些高危瞬间？

* **加盟签约前夕**：对方提供了漂亮的单店盈利模型和官方背书，你想彻底验明项目背后的供应链加价与快招套路。
* **刚开店即遇冷**：开业两周冲高，随后客流急速塌陷。你在怀疑产品、位置与选址经理的承诺。
* **处在连续亏损期**：周围人劝你“餐饮有养店期，多守几个月”，你在犹豫止损离场还是继续垫资。
* **准备接手转让铺**：现老板声称盈利稳定、因家庭原因低价转让，你想查验真实的账目与动迁隐患。

## 勇哥核心商业判断规律

* **建店如豪掷，营销如乞丐**  
  花 50 万砸高档装修，推广开支预算 500 元。好产品无人知晓，根源是营销认知脱节。
* **开业前两月营收，打七折才是真实水准**  
  “养店期”大多是自我安慰。好店铺第一天就具备留客能力。连续亏损超半年不退，是沉没成本在作祟。
* **奶茶、咖啡、快餐属于品牌先行品类**  
  顾客出门前就已经想好买哪家。在同一地段开没有心智认知的杂牌，没有任何生存胜率。
* **亏损超过半年的“坚持”，本质上是麻木**  
  果断止损比借贷补仓更考量认知能力。

## Why This AI Skill Exists

You stumble upon a restaurant franchise opportunity online.  
The sales rep highlights surging market demand. The site-selection broker points out an apparently bustling street. The launch playbook is already printed.  
Yet you feel a quiet unease—and you cannot pinpoint where the trap lies.

This Skill exists to diagnose that unease with concrete data and historical patterns.

Sun Jiyong ("Yongge") conducts six hours of live consultations daily on Bilibili, reviewing failed and failing restaurants. He speaks with first-time operators who lost $100,000 without understanding why, single mothers drained by franchise shell companies, and buyers deceived by inflated POS ledgers.

He is not a textbook instructor. He has inspected thousands of financial casualties.  
This Skill codifies lessons from **645 verified consultations**, turning repeatable failure patterns into a structured AI decision engine.

## The Four Stages of Franchise Scams

1. **Bait**  
   You search for an established brand online and land on an authorized-partner portal. When you call, the agent claims you are competing for the final regional territory.
2. **Lock**  
   You travel to corporate headquarters and enter a polished showroom. Signatures are celebrated with applause. The rep pushes: "Sign today and save $8,000 on upfront fees."
3. **Squeeze**  
   Once funds clear, the assigned leasing broker steers you toward retail units next to flagship stores, quietly taking a spread on sub-leased rent.
4. **Dump**  
   Three months later, when working capital evaporates, corporate support delivers its standard defense: "Our model works. Your execution failed."

---

## Critical Moments to Run This Skill

* **Before signing franchise agreements**: Audit corporate claims, markups on mandated supplies, and red flags of shell companies.
* **When foot traffic drops post-launch**: Diagnose whether the drop stems from weak product differentiation, flawed location fundamentals, or artificial opening hype.
* **During sustained cash-flow deficits**: Determine mathematically whether to restructure pricing or execute an immediate lease exit before compounding losses.
* **Before buying an existing restaurant**: Cross-examine POS order histories, utility bills, and lease agreements to uncover fake revenue or pending zoning shifts.

---

## Core Operational Rules

* **Over-investing in decor, under-investing in reach**  
  Spending $80,000 on custom furnishings while allocating $80 for customer acquisition guarantees failure.
* **Discount opening-month revenue by 30%**  
  The "store warmup period" is largely a myth. Strong concepts convert foot traffic immediately. Sustained losses across six months signal structural flaws.
* **Brand-first categories forgive no generic clones**  
  In bubble tea, coffee, and fast food, consumers pick the brand before leaving home. An unknown nameplate in the same retail strip has almost zero survival odds.
